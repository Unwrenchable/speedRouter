Security & Performance Optimiser

Applies the following recommended settings to your modem in one click:

  • DNS → Cloudflare 1.1.1.1 / Google 8.8.8.8 (faster + private)
  • Disable TR-069 / CWMP — stops ISP remote management of your router
  • Enable Firewall & SPI
  • Disable UPnP (common attack vector)
  • Set MTU to 1500 for optimal throughput
  • Disable WPS (PIN brute-force vulnerability)
